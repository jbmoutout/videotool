v1.0.0
2026-01-11

1. You’re building a local, transcript-first tool for talking-heavy streamers, not a traditional video editor.
2. The core pain is subject sprawl and digression in long streams, not repetition or highlights.
3. The tool extracts distinct topics from a stream and lets the creator pick one coherent thread.
4. Output is a reversible cut plan — never auto-deletions or black-box edits.
5. Design principle: suggest, don’t assume, with explainable decisions at every step.
6. Context depth comes from RAG + a creator-controlled Lore/Glossary, not hidden training.
7. Strong OSS / open-core ethos: transparent logic, portable outputs, no lock-in.
8. Explicit goal is to avoid closed systems that capture data, workflows, or creative control.
9. MVP is CLI-first, Python-based, optimized for fast iteration and agentic coding.
10. Validation target is clarity: “this is the video this stream wants to be”, not automation or growth.
