How it works

VideoTool runs a 3-step pipeline on any stream VOD:

1.
Ingest — downloads the video (Twitch URL or local file), extracts audio
2.
Transcribe — sends audio to Groq Whisper, returns full transcript
3.
Detect beats — Claude reads the full transcript, returns topics + narrative beats with timestamps and confidence scores

The output is a beat map: highlight, core, context, chat, transition, break — for every second of your stream.

The app

Paste a Twitch VOD link or drop a video file. The pipeline runs and shows an interactive beat timeline with click-to-seek video playback. You see every topic, every narrative arc, with confidence scores.
